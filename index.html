Server Rules

Lifesteal Minecraft Server & Discord Community Guidelines

Lifesteal Gameplay Note

This is a Lifesteal server where griefing, trapping, and PvP are allowed as core gameplay mechanics. However, there are still rules to ensure fair play and a positive community experience.

Lifesteal Mechanics

Rules regarding the core lifesteal gameplay

  • Losing all hearts results in a temporary ban until reset
  • Griefing and raiding are allowed as part of gameplay
  • Trapping players is permitted but must follow server guidelines
  • Stealing items and resources is part of the game

Fair Play

Ensuring a fair experience for all players

  • No hacked clients or unfair advantages
  • No exploiting game bugs or glitches
  • Respect the server performance - no lag machines
  • No inappropriate skins or usernames

Player Conduct

How to interact with other players

  • No excessive harassment or toxic behavior
  • Respect all players regardless of skill level
  • No hate speech, racism, or discrimination
  • English only in public chats

Chat Etiquette

Guidelines for communication in text channels

  • Be respectful to all members and staff
  • No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps
  • Keep conversations in appropriate channels
  • No advertising without permission
  • English only in public channels

Voice Channels

Rules for voice communication

  • No earrape, soundboards, or excessive noise
  • Respect others when they're speaking
  • Move to appropriate channels for different activities
  • Staff can move or disconnect you if necessary

General Conduct

Overall behavior expectations

  • No NSFW, offensive, or inappropriate content
  • Respect staff decisions and follow their instructions
  • No sharing personal information
  • Use common sense and be kind to others
